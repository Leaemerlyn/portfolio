Executive Summmary

Public art is meant to be readily accessible to everyone and increase livability within the city, yet one of the biggest issues that the public faces is that there are barriers preventing viewers from interacting with the art piece, leading the public to not conversing about it with other viewers orseeking to give their feedback to artists. Here we present to you an interactive application using augmented reality to encourage users to physically go to the art site and unlock unique commentsmade by the artist and to interact with the piece by commenting and/or drawing on it.
