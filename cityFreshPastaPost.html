City Fresh Pasta Site

For my marketing project, my group has to propose a marketing plan for a Pittsburgh based company. We chose City Fresh Pasta. When I saw their website, I thought that there could be several improvements. This is their old website:





This is how I would change the website:


I chose to go with the theme colors green and pasta yellow because of its existing logo. I thought that having theme colors will make it easier for customers to remember this restaurant.

For this project, I learned how to make the page scroll. When I was making the menu page, I knew I wanted to include pictures. Not just one picture, but as many pictures as customers need to decide if they want the item. I learned that cutting things off makes it clear for the user to know that there is more content, which makes them scroll.

I used the fix position when scrolling option for the menu bar. I noticed that many websites have this, and I thought that this is an easy way for users to have everything in one place even when they scroll down to view more content.
